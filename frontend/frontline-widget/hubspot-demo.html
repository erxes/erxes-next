üéØ How to Test the Widget

  1. Look for the chat bubble: You'll see a blue chat icon in the bottom-right corner
  2. Notice the welcome tooltip: A small message appears to encourage interaction
  3. Click to open: The widget slides up with a smooth animation
  4. Try conversation starters: Click on any of the suggested topics
  5. Send messages: Type in the input field and press Enter or click send
  6. Watch typing indicators: See animated dots when the bot is "typing"
  7. Check timestamps: Each message shows the time it was sent
  8. Test responsiveness: Resize your browser to see mobile adaptation
